Rotary China

Message from RI China Chief Representative

徐乃达 Richard Zi-国际扶轮 中国首席代表
Rotary International - China Representative

I had the privilege of joining Rotary International 42 years ago. During the 42 years, I was deeply moved by Rotary principles - No politics, No racism, No religion, Serve the community. Rotary International will continue to follow those principles and contribute to the local community. As volunteers, we respect the local cultures and follow the guidelines of the Chinese government.

The spread of the pandemic around the world aﬀects every club member and family, it has changed the life pattern that we are used to. Every leader of the club should be caring for the health of its members and family as a top priority.

Disease prevention and treatment are one of the seven focus areas of Rotary International, during the past 40 years, Rotary International led the global vaccine for polio which cause Polio is now almost Destroyed around the world. I urge every member of China to continue helping our local communities during this pandemic, show the values of Rotary International.

扶轮宗旨

Object of Rotary



扶轮之宗旨在于鼓励并培养以服务精神为可贵事业之基础，尤其注重于鼓励与培养：

The object of Rotary is to encourage and foster the ideal of service as basis of worthy enterprise and, in particular to encourage and foster:

第一：藉增广交游，为扩展服务之机会。

First: the development acquaintance as an opportunity for service.

第二：在各种事业及专门职务中，提高道德之标准，认识一切有益于社会的业务之价值，及每一扶轮社员应尊重其本身之业务，藉以服务社会。

Second: high ethical standards in business and professions; the recognition of the worthiness of all useful occupations; and the dignifying by each Rotarian of his occupation as an opportunity to serve society.
