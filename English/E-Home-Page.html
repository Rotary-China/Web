Rotary China

The Covid-19 affects everyone across borders. Strive on, Wuhan! Strive on, China!

The new Covid-19 outbreak was unexpected and its development exceeded everyone's expectations. Many Rotarians are even stranded abroad and unable to return home. Rotary China is concerned about the safety of all Rotarians and their families. Please cooperate with the Government by not going to any public places with a large gathering, cancelling fellowships temporarily, wearing a mask and washing your hands frequently. If you are feeling sick, please seek medical attention immediately but if you need further help, you are welcome to contact Rotary China.

Currently, many Rotary clubs have contributed money or donated medical supplies. Once again, I am appealing to all Rotary Clubs and Rotarians in China and abroad to donate within their ability, medical-related items that are lacking (such as gloves, masks, protective clothing, disinfectant, etc). We believe that the outbreak will gradually slow down under the leadership of the government and once again call upon everyone to cooperate with the local government's pandemic prevention measures, and let us work together to overcome the pandemic.

扶轮宗旨

Object of Rotary



扶轮之宗旨在于鼓励并培养以服务精神为可贵事业之基础，尤其注重于鼓励与培养：

The object of Rotary is to encourage and foster the ideal of service as basis of worthy enterprise and, in particular to encourage and foster:

第一：藉增广交游，为扩展服务之机会。

First: the development acquaintance as an opportunity for service.

第二：在各种事业及专门职务中，提高道德之标准，认识一切有益于社会的业务之价值，及每一扶轮社员应尊重其本身之业务，藉以服务社会。

Second: high ethical standards in business and professions; the recognition of the worthiness of all useful occupations; and the dignifying by each Rotarian of his occupation as an opportunity to serve society.
